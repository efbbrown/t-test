T-test calculator

Use this calculator to test for a difference in the population means of two samples using an unpaired t-test.

Assumptions
  • The samples are independent of one another.
  • If sample sizes are < 15, their distributions should suggest normality.
  • If sample sizes are < 45, they should show no skewness or include outliers.
Common Applications
  • Health - Comparing responses to treatment between test groups.
  • Market Research - Comparing focus group responses to products.
  • Manufacturing - Comparing product quality accross machinery, factories or proceses.
